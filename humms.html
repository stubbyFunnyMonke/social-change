Harmony in Verse

  • Environmental Poetry Creation
  • Join Event
    Before Time
  • Participate Now
×

Dive into the poetic realm, where your words become the brushstrokes painting the beauty of nature. Craft verses that capture the essence of landscapes, the dance of seasons, and the whispers of the wind.

Unearth the soulful reflections within you and pen verses that delve into the intricate relationship between humanity and the environment. Let your poetry echo the urgency of conservation, portraying the interconnectedness of all living things.

Sail through the vast seas of inspiration as you channel your words to celebrate the ocean's majesty and lament its plight. Your verses will be a powerful vessel conveying the need for marine conservation and the fragility of aquatic ecosystems.

Watch as the sun dips below the horizon, casting a golden glow on the world. Write sonnets that capture the transient beauty of sunsets, reminding readers of the delicate balance that sustains life on Earth.

Let your verses climb the branches of metaphorical trees, celebrating the majesty of forests and highlighting the importance of reforestation in poetic narratives that resonate with hearts.

Explore the transient beauty of landscapes, from verdant meadows to rugged mountains. Craft poems that convey the urgency of preserving these delicate ecosystems, embracing the role of stewards for future generations.

Infuse your verses with emotion, creating a poetic tapestry that communicates the joys and sorrows of our environmental journey. Let your words inspire change and foster a deeper connection with the world around us.

The most evocative and impactful poems will receive recognition, with select pieces having the opportunity to be featured in environmental campaigns, spreading the power of your words far and wide.

Submission Deadline: December 15, 2023. Embrace the challenge, Humms poets! Let your verses sing the song of nature and become a lyrical force for positive change.
