Calling all students with a passion for excellence and a flair for creativity! Whether you're a future scientist, an aspiring artist, a budding entrepreneur, or a literary genius, this challenge is for YOU!

Embrace the opportunity to showcase your unique talents, regardless of your academic background! From the science whiz to the poetry prodigy, everyone is invited to join the excitement.

It's not just about showcasing your skills; it's about winning the hearts of your peers! Share your entry with your friends, family, and fellow students. The more likes your submission gets, the closer you are to victory!

The stakes are high, and so is the reward! The entry with the most likes at the end of the competition will walk away with the coveted Grand Prize!

Express yourself in any form you desire - be it a captivating essay, a mind-blowing science experiment, an enchanting piece of art, or a business pitch that could change the game. The possibilities are endless!

Hurry! The clock is ticking. Submissions are welcome until December 15, 2023. Winners will be announced shortly after.

Objectives

Embark on a transformative artistic journey with MMA students as they take on the challenge of creating a distinctive and environmentally friendly 3D art piece. This project not only seeks to foster creativity within the realm of mixed martial arts but also instills a profound sense of responsibility toward sustainability by utilizing recycled materials. Furthermore, the art piece aims to serve as a powerful medium for raising awareness about the environmental crisis.
